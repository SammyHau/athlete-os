# Design System

## Direction
Apple clarity combined with Canyon precision.

## Personality
- Calm
- Technical
- Premium
- Athletic
- Confident

## Colors
- Background: #F4F4F1
- Surface: #FFFFFF
- Text: #111111
- Secondary text: #74746F
- Accent: #B8FF38

## Spacing
4, 8, 12, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64

## Radius
12, 18, 24, 28, 32

## Rules
- Generous whitespace
- No unnecessary gradients
- Very few colors
- Large, clear typography
- Strong contrast
- Subtle borders instead of heavy shadows
